Who We Are

Building Futures,
One Child at a Time

Rose Cottage School is a private nursery and primary school at No. 3, Haastrup Oroazi/Eligbam Link Road, Mile 4, Port Harcourt. We provide a safe, stimulating environment where children learn, grow, and discover their potential.

Our curriculum blends academic rigor with strong character development — preparing children not just for exams, but for life.

  • Safe, secure, and cheerful learning environment
  • Trained and caring teachers for every class level
  • Strong emphasis on diction and communication
  • Sound moral and etiquette training
  • After-school care and drop-off services available
  • Career-awareness and creative performance events
